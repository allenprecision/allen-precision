Odoo Website Grouped Product

A Grouped Product is a combination of simple products. You can group any number of simple/virtual items and display the grouped product as one entry on the website.

Key Features

Create your own grouped product.
Manage Group Items.
One click ADD TO CART of group items.
Manage Pricelist and Inventory for group items.
Presenting a grouped product can create an incentive for customers to purchase additional items.
A grouped product provides an easy way to offer variations of a product, and list them all on the same page.
Although they are presented as a group, each product in the group is purchased as a separate item.
In the shopping cart, each item and the quantity purchased appears a separate line item.

How it Works?

Create your own grouped product. You can create any simple product as a grouped product and Manage Group Items.
One click ADD TO CART of multiple group items with different quantities.
Manage Pricelist and Inventory for group items (simple products defined under group product).
